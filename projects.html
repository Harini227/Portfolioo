A Multi Robot Decision Intelligence

An AI-powered system enabling coordinated decision-making across multiple autonomous robots.

Project Overview

This project was developed during an intense 24-hour hackathon, where our team tackled the challenge of making multiple robots work together intelligently without human intervention. The system applies basic AI-based decision-making logic to enable each robot to assess its environment, communicate with its peers, and collectively decide on the best course of action.

The core idea is to simulate how a team of autonomous agents can dynamically coordinate tasks, avoid conflicts, and achieve a shared goal — much like how a well-organized human team operates. This has powerful real-world applications in warehouse automation, search-and-rescue operations, and smart manufacturing.

Key Features
Real-time Decision Making
Robots evaluate and respond to dynamic situations instantly without delays.
Multi-Robot Coordination
Seamless communication and collaboration between multiple autonomous agents.
Task Allocation
Smart distribution of tasks based on each robot's capabilities and position.
AI-Based Logic
Decision trees and rule-based AI to guide robot behaviors intelligently.
